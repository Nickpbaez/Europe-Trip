6 How to use this

Workflow for the group.

1
Book something? Drop the PDF (or screenshot) into the correct Drive folder. Name it clearly so anyone can find it.
2
Want it to show up on this page? Send the PDF + Drive share link to Claude — it'll read the file, update the page, and hand back a new HTML to re-upload.
3
Need something on the road? The Drive folders work offline on your phone if you star them in the Google Drive app ahead of time. Do this before leaving wifi.
